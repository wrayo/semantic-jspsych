You just completed a semantic fluency task. In cognitive psychology, that means we give you a category and look at which ideas come to mind, how many you generate, and how quickly they appear.

Here is the exciting part: when we combine responses across PSC 1 students, we can build a mind map of introductory psychology. That map helps us see which concepts are most available, which ideas cluster together, and how the class's knowledge network is organized.

In other words, your brain dump becomes data that helps us visualize how psychology lives in memory. Very cool, very science-of-thinking.
